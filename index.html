Last year

For Augusta, this is our fouth robotics competition, and most certainly not our last competition. Last year however we didn't do as well as we had hoped. However this year with a new strategy and new teammates as well as more experience in robotics we hope to do better than last year and excel in the competition.

This Year

This year Augusta is completely changing it up. With new leadership and new members on our team we will be a competitive team this year by focusing more on the design of the robot and getting it completed by practice day. We're also focusing on making a more dedicated notebook team that is constantly documenting everything. This years team will be fierce competitor in the B.E.S.T. Robotics program whilst at the same time introducing lifelong skills that we will use for the rest of our lives.
